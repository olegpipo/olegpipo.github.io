Welcome to the PanicVault user manual. Everything the app can do is documented here, chapter by chapter, for both iPhone and Mac — from creating your first vault to wiring up AutoFill, TOTP codes, passkeys, and sync.

If you are brand new, start with **Getting Started** and work down. If you already have a vault and just need one answer — how to import from another password manager, how to turn on the browser extension, which keyboard shortcut does what — jump straight to the chapter you need. Every chapter stands on its own.

Your passwords live in a standard KeePass `.kdbx` file that you own. Nothing in this manual asks you to trust a server, and nothing here is locked behind a subscription.
