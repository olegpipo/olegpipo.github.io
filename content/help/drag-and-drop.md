On Mac, PanicVault supports drag and drop for quick reorganization of both entries and groups. It is the fastest way to file an entry away or rearrange your folder structure without opening any menus.

## Dragging Entries

- Drag an entry from the entry list onto any group in the sidebar to move it
- To move multiple entries at once, first select them (hold **Cmd** and click — see [Bulk Operations](/help/bulk-operations/)), then drag the selection onto the target group

## Dragging Groups

- Drag a group in the sidebar onto another group to make it a subgroup
- Drag a group onto the **GROUPS** header at the top of the sidebar to move it back to the root level
- Drag and drop is the quickest way to restructure your group hierarchy

For the menu-based alternatives to these actions, see [Groups & Folders](/help/groups/).
