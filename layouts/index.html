Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Real ownership of your credentials. No proprietary cloud, no account to create, no subscription fee.

KeePass (.kdbx 4) Compatibility

Open and save vaults created by KeePass, KeePassXC, KeePassDX, Strongbox, and more. Entry history, custom fields, attachments, group hierarchy, custom icons, and recycle bin are fully supported.

iCloud Drive & Google Drive Sync

Sync your vault across iPhone, iPad, and Mac through iCloud Drive or Google Drive. Smart entry-level merging preserves changes from every device. Conflicts are shown side by side so you pick the version you want. Sign in to multiple Google accounts and sync different vaults with different accounts.

Passkeys (WebAuthn)

Store and manage passkeys alongside your passwords. Compatible with the KeePassXC passkey format for full cross-platform interoperability.

AutoFill Integration

Fill usernames and passwords in Safari and apps with the system AutoFill credential provider. Override URLs let you match entries to sites with different login domains.

Built-in Authenticator (TOTP)

Generate two-factor authentication codes right alongside your passwords. Scan a QR code or enter the secret manually. Compatible with all standard TOTP formats.

Password & Passphrase Generator

Create strong random passwords or memorable passphrases from the EFF wordlist. A live strength meter shows you exactly how secure each password is.

Biometric Unlock

Unlock with Face ID or Touch ID. Your master password is stored in the Secure Enclave keychain — never transmitted anywhere.

Offline First

Every feature works without an internet connection. Cloud sync is optional and happens only when you choose to enable it.

Bulk Operations & Drag and Drop

Select multiple entries at once to move or delete them in bulk. On Mac, drag entries between groups and drag groups to reorganize your hierarchy.

Organize with Groups & Tags

Create nested folders to sort your entries. Reorder and customize groups with notes and custom icons. Add tags for cross-cutting categories. Search across titles, usernames, URLs, and notes instantly.

Favicon Auto-Fetch

Tap a button to automatically download a website's icon and set it as the entry's custom icon — the quickest way to make entries recognizable at a glance.

Default Vault & Keyboard Shortcuts

Set a default vault that opens automatically on launch. On Mac, use full keyboard shortcuts for search, new entries, password generation, locking, and more.

Entry History & Attachments

Every password change is saved automatically. Attach files like recovery codes, certificates, or documents to any entry.

Custom Colors & Icons

Personalize entries with text color, background color, and 69 built-in KeePass icons or your own custom images.

100% Test Coverage

Every line of code is tested and verified. Full test coverage means fewer bugs, faster fixes, and confidence that your vault works exactly as expected.
